Brand Info

About easy2print

Our Story

easy2print started with a simple idea: personalized printing should be easy. We noticed that many people love creative designs but feel overwhelmed by complicated ordering processes or struggle to find high-quality digital files.

So we built a place that combines both: a rich library of digital assets for makers who want to print at home or with their cutting machines, and a print-on-demand (POD) service for those who prefer to receive a finished, professionally printed product.

Our Mission

To democratize design and personalized printing. Anyone, anywhere, should be able to create something unique – for themselves or as a gift – without hassle.

What We Value

Simplicity: From browsing to downloading or ordering, every step is designed to be intuitive and fast.

Quality: Every digital file is tested for compatibility. Every physical product is inspected to ensure vibrant colors and long-lasting results.

Creativity & Community: We work with independent designers to bring you fresh, original artwork that stands out from mass-produced goods.

The Team

We are a small group of designers, printing enthusiasts, and tech lovers. Behind easy2print is a team that genuinely enjoys making things and helping others bring their ideas to life.

Quality Policy

Our Quality Commitment

At easy2print, quality is not just a promise – it’s a standard we uphold for every product, whether digital or physical.

For Digital Files

  • Compatibility Check: All SVG, DXF, PNG, and EPS files are tested to work smoothly with popular software (Cricut Design Space, Silhouette Studio, Adobe Illustrator, CorelDRAW, Inkscape).
  • Resolution: Raster files (PNG, JPG) are provided at a minimum of 300 DPI, ensuring sharp, print-ready quality.
  • Layered & Organized: Multi-layer designs have clearly named layers for easy editing.
  • Technical Support: If you encounter any issue with a file, contact us within 24 hours – we’ll help you resolve it promptly.

For Print-on-Demand Products

  • Materials: We use eco-friendly inks, high-grade cotton (for apparel), durable ceramic (for mugs), and premium canvas to ensure lasting beauty.
  • Printing Process: Each item is individually printed and inspected by experienced technicians before packaging.
  • Packaging: Orders are carefully packed to prevent damage during transit, so they arrive in perfect condition.

Your Feedback Matters

If you receive a product that does not match the description or has a quality issue, please contact us with photos. We will arrange a replacement or refund according to our return policy.

Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy and download digital files?

  • Add the desired design to your cart and complete checkout.
  • After payment, you’ll receive a confirmation email with a download link.
  • You can also access your files anytime under “My Account” → “Orders”.

How do I order a printed product?

  • Choose the product type (T-shirt, mug, canvas, etc.).
  • Select size, color, and any customization options.
  • Add to cart and checkout.
  • If you need a specific design or personalization, leave a note during checkout.

What software do I need to open SVG files?

  • Cricut Design Space, Silhouette Studio (Designer Edition+), Adobe Illustrator, CorelDRAW, Inkscape (free).

Can I use digital files for commercial purposes?

Each product description states its license. Most designs allow commercial use (selling physical items), but reselling the digital file is prohibited.

What are the processing and shipping times?

  • Digital files: Instant download.
  • Printed products: Processing 2-5 days. Shipping: Domestic (3-7 days), International (7-15 days).

Need more help?

Email us at **support@easy2print.com** – we usually reply within 24 hours.
