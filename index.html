Brand Info

About Easy Embroidery

Our Story

Easy Embroidery started with a simple idea: high-quality machine embroidery should be accessible to everyone. We noticed that many hobbyists with home machines (Brother, Janome, Singer) struggle to find well-digitized files that don't cause thread breaks or nesting.

So we built a dedicated library of premium digital assets (DST, PES, EMB) that are professionally tested on home embroidery machines before being listed.

Our Mission

To empower home embroidery enthusiasts by providing reliable, beautiful, and easy-to-stitch designs. We want you to spend more time stitching and less time troubleshooting.

What We Value

Precision: Every design is digitized with optimal density and pathing for a smooth experience.

Beginner Friendly: We include quality reports and stitching guides to help those just starting their embroidery journey.

Community: We listen to our users to create the floral, animal, and monogram patterns you actually want to stitch.

The Team

We are a small group of digitizing experts and embroidery lovers. Behind Easy Embroidery is a team that understands the joy of a perfectly finished project.

Quality Policy

Our Quality Commitment

At Easy Embroidery, quality means a design that stitches perfectly from start to finish.

Our Digitizing Standards

  • Machine Compatibility: All designs are provided in industry-standard formats (DST, PES, JEF, XXX, HUS) and tested on popular home embroidery machines.
  • Optimal Pathing: We minimize jump stitches and ensure logical color changes to save you time and thread.
  • Density Control: Patterns are balanced to ensure high coverage without being too heavy for common fabrics like cotton or linen.
  • Quality Reports: We provide recommended stabilizer types and needle sizes for every premium design.

Support

If you encounter any issue with a file (thread nesting, alignment), contact us. We offer free design adjustments if the file doesn't meet our strict quality standards.

Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy and download digital files?

  • Add the desired design to your cart and complete checkout.
  • After payment, you’ll receive a confirmation email with a download link.
  • You can also access your files anytime under “My Account” → “Orders”.

How do I order a printed product?

  • Choose the product type (T-shirt, mug, canvas, etc.).
  • Select size, color, and any customization options.
  • Add to cart and checkout.
  • If you need a specific design or personalization, leave a note during checkout.

What software do I need to open SVG files?

  • Cricut Design Space, Silhouette Studio (Designer Edition+), Adobe Illustrator, CorelDRAW, Inkscape (free).

Can I use digital files for commercial purposes?

Each product description states its license. Most designs allow commercial use (selling physical items), but reselling the digital file is prohibited.

What are the processing and shipping times?

  • Digital files: Instant download.
  • Printed products: Processing 2-5 days. Shipping: Domestic (3-7 days), International (7-15 days).
